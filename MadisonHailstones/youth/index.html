Madison GAA is hosting a Summer Youth Clinic for Boys and Girls ages 4-16. Learn the skills of Gaelic football, hurling and camogie in a fun environment.

There is no cost for this clinic and we encourage all kids with an interest in learning about these sports to try it out.

The clinics will take place at University Bay fields during June, July and August. Days and times are still to be determined.

Please reach out to info@madisonhurling.com with any questions or if you'd like to express interest.
