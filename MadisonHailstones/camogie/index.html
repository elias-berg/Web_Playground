Ladies Gaelic Football

The fastest sport on grass, Camogie is the women's equivalent of hurling with a few minor rule changes. Described best as a combination of softball, lacrosse, and field hockey; this sport requires hand-eye coordination with a hurley to strike a ball up the field in order to score.

2019 was the first year of an official practice schedule for our Camogie team as we slowly grow this sport in Madison. Equipment is provided at practices and we welcome new players throughout the year.
