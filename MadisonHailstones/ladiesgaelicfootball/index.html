The fastest growing team sport in Europe, Ladies Gaelic Football is best described as a cross between soccer, volleyball, and basketball. This sport combines the skills of carrying, bouncing, kicking, hand-passing, and toe-kicking (or soloing) a ball up the field to score on your opponents.

After officially registering in 2017, our Madison Ladies Gaelic Football team has grown throughout the years to include a roster of players from all types of sports backgrounds.

All new players welcome!
